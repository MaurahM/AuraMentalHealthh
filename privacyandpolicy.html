Our Commitment to User Safety

Crisis Detection

Our AI uses advanced sentiment analysis to identify "at-risk" language. If a crisis is detected, users are immediately routed to human-verified hotlines.

Safe-Zone Chat

Aura is programmed to decline requests for harmful, illegal, or sexually explicit content, maintaining a clean and therapeutic environment.

3. How We Secure the Data

To protect both the users and the company, we utilize a "Double-Blind" data policy:

Encrypted Storage

All conversations are encrypted. Even in the unlikely event of a system breach, the data is unreadable without the master security keys.

Anonymized Identities

We decouple personal metadata from chat logs. Our internal analytics see "User #8822" instead of your name or email.

4. Legal Compliance

Aura operates in compliance with international data protection standards. We reserve the right to cooperate with local authorities only in cases where there is a clear, immediate threat to life (Mandatory Reporting) as required by law.
