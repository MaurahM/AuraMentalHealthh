Frequently Asked Questions

Is my conversation really private?
Yes. Aura uses end-to-end encryption. Your conversations are anonymized, and even our developers cannot link your chats back to your personal identity.
How does the emergency contact system work?
If Aura detects specific keywords indicating self-harm or crisis, it will trigger a prompt asking if you'd like to reach out to your "Trusted Contacts" or local emergency hotlines immediately.
Can Aura understand my local language?
Aura is designed to be culturally intelligent. It understands English, Swahili, Sheng, and many local dialects to ensure you can express yourself comfortably.
